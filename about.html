How I Work

  • Market Knowledge

    I'll walk you through competitor homes in person. You'll see what buyers see—the condition, layout, upgrades, and pricing. This removes the guesswork.

  • Strategic Pricing

    Your home is priced based on recent sales data and current buyer behavior. I provide market updates every 30 days, so you always know where you stand.

  • Professional Presentation

    High-quality photos, staging guidance, and clean marketing materials. First impressions happen online, so we get it right.

  • Clear Communication

    Weekly updates on showings and buyer feedback. Direct answers to your questions. You're never left wondering what's happening.

  • Negotiation

    I qualify buyers before presenting offers, review terms carefully, and navigate inspection and appraisal issues with your best interests in mind.
